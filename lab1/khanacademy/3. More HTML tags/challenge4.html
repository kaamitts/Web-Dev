Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 24 cookies
Ingredient Quantity
All-purpose flour 2 1/4 cups
Butter 1 cup
Brown sugar 3/4 cup
Granulated sugar 3/4 cup
Chocolate chips 2 cups
Eggs 2 large
Vanilla extract 1 tsp

Steps:

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).

Step 2: Cream the butter and sugars together until light and fluffy.

Step 3: Add eggs and vanilla extract, then mix well.

Step 4: Gradually add flour, then fold in chocolate chips.

Step 5: Scoop dough onto a baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes.

Source: AllRecipes

Spaghetti Carbonara

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredient Quantity
Spaghetti 400 grams
Egg yolks 4
Parmesan cheese 1/2 cup
Pancetta 150 grams
Black pepper to taste

Steps:

Step 1: Cook spaghetti until al dente.

Step 2: Fry pancetta until crispy.

Step 3: Whisk egg yolks with Parmesan cheese and pepper.

Step 4: Combine pasta with pancetta and remove from heat.

Step 5: Quickly mix in egg mixture to create a creamy sauce.

Source: Giallo Zafferano

Avocado Toast

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredient Quantity
Bread slices 2
Avocado 1 large
Lemon juice 1 tsp
Salt to taste
Red chili flakes optional

Steps:

Step 1: Toast the bread slices until golden brown.

Step 2: Mash the avocado with lemon juice and salt.

Step 3: Spread the avocado mixture on the toast.

Step 4: Sprinkle red chili flakes if desired and serve.

Source: Love and Lemons
